Introducing

The world of non-fungible tokens (NFTs) is rapidly evolving, and with it comes the need for powerful and intuitive tools to help investors and collectors navigate this complex market. Easyzoom is a revolutionary NFT analysis tool that provides advanced analytics and visualization capabilities, allowing users to easily understand and interpret the data.

With Easyzoom, you can identify trends and patterns in the market, and make more informed decisions about when to buy and sell your NFTs. Easyzoom is simple, fast, and powerful, with a wide range of charts and analytics that provide a deep dive into sales history, floor prices, volumes, and trends. With the exclusive whale tracking features, you can discover the NFTs biggest players and see which NFTs they are buying and selling. With the live feed data, you can stay on top of the NFT market keeping track of every sale, transfer, and mint, and get instant alerts when important events occur. With the wallet history feature, you can easily sift through each wallet to see which NFTs are being bought and sold, and watch as the profits (loss) roll in.

Easyzoom is more than just a tool, it's a community. The members of the Easyzoom DAO have a direct say in which features are needed and how the tool evolves. By minting your own Easyzoom pass, you'll not only have a role in the improvement of the tool but also be rewarded for every future subscription enjoying a tangible reward (staking system). The DAO council will vote on which features to add, prioritize development, and decide on the direction of the tool, making it truly community driven.

By joining the exclusive DAO, you'll have access to the community wallet, decision-making processes, and profit-sharing system. The community wallet is funded by a portion of proceeds from mint, secondary sales royalties, subscription service revenue and profits generated from flipped NFTs, ensuring stability and growth for the community. Additional details can be found in the tokenomics & funding section.

Tokenomics & Funding

The tokenomics and funding structure of Easyzoom are designed to support the growth and development of the tool, as well as reward members of the community for their participation and contributions.

he total supply of Easyzoom tokens is limited to 888, with each token representing a lifetime pass to access the Easyzoom tool and the community-driven DAO. The mint price is of 0.088 ETH and the royalties for secondary market transactions are set at 10%.

  1. Breakdown of Community Wallet funding
    • Mint proceeds: 30% from the mint of Easyzoom passes at a price of 0.088 ETH each, with a total supply of 888 passes.
    • Secondary sales royalties: 70% from the revenue of 10% royalties on secondary market sales.
    • Subscription service revenues: 70% from the ongoing subscription service for the Easyzoom tool,
    • Flipping proceeds: 100% from the flipping of high-potential NFT projects identified and voted on trough the Community Governance.

The remaining funds at each of the points are dedicated to the team and marketing. The transparent funding breakdown not only showcases the sustainability of the project, but also emphasizes the community-driven nature of Easyzoom, with a significant portion of funds allocated towards the development and improvement of the tool as well as the community as guided by the members of the DAO council, the Easyzoom’s passes holders.

Subscription Service Revenue
  1. Individual Subscription

    The launch of an individual subscription service will allow users to access to Easyzoom, on a paid basis. This subscription will be suitable for individual investors and collectors looking for a high quality NFTs analysis tool (and more in the future). The subscription fee will be a fixed amount, the value of which will be determined prior to launch and will be charged monthly.

  2. Collection Subscription

    Easyzoom will also offer a collection subscription service for the holders of other NFT collections. This subscription will provide access to the tool to the holders of the collection in question, these collections will be incentivized in order to offer an additional utility to their community. The subscription fee for this service will be determined on a case-by-case basis, based on the size and value of the collection. This subscription service will generate significant revenue for the DAO. NFT collections interested in this service will be evaluated individually.

    Requirements: collection floor price > individual subscription monthly fee. If the NFT collection interested in the service does not meet this requirement, access to the tool to holders of this collection cannot be provided.

    It is important to note that these services do not make the users of these services members of the DAO. Only holders of the Easyzoom passes are members of the DAO.

DAO

The Easyzoom DAO council is a decentralized autonomous organization that allows members to have a direct say in the development and evolution of the tool. The council is made up of token holders, who can vote on proposals for new features and updates to the tool, as well as participate in the decision-making processes of the community.

To become a member of the DAO council, individuals must hold at least one Easyzoom token, which grants them access to the council and its benefits. Members can participate in the decision-making process by proposing new features and updates for the tool, as well as by voting on proposals put forth by other members.

The community wallet, which is managed by the DAO, receives funds as described in the Tokenomics & Funding section. These funds are then used to perform operations as decided by the members of the DAO and distributed among the token holders, allowing them to share in the success of the community

The transparency of the funds of the DAO is key, all transactions are discussed transparently within the discord server, allowing members to stay informed and involved. In addition, members have the right to attend community meetings for the purpose of directly impacting the future of the DAO.

A clear and easily accessible dashboard will be available for members to track all transactions, funds, and activities of the DAO. This will always ensure complete transparency and oversight.

Staking

Staking is a key aspect of the Easyzoom DAO, as it allows members of the community to earn rewards for their participation in the project. By staking their passes, members are committing to the success of the project and will be incentivized to actively participate and contribute to its growth.

To implement this system, Easyzoom will make use of a staking system that will be based on a quarterly or biannual basis. This will allow for regular rewards those will be distributed based on the number of passes held and staked by members, providing an incentive to actively participate in the growth of the project and hold onto their passes.

The profits generated by the DAO will come from a variety of sources which are explained in detail in the tokenomics and funding section.

The staking system is designed to be simple and easy to understand for anyone. It will be transparently communicated and updated through our communication channels (Website, Twitter, Discord), so that DAO members can stay informed and up to date on the status of their staked passes and rewards.

Overall, the staking system is a crucial aspect of Easyzoom, it provides a sustainable way to reward and engage the community and aligns the interests of members with the success of the project.

Schedule

With a limited number of 888 passes, the release of the lifetime NFT passes will create a sense of exclusivity and scarcity, making them highly valuable for the members of the community.
  • Launch of the already built NFT analysis tool: After the release of the lifetime NFT passes, we will launch the NFT analysis tool, providing the community with a fast and powerful tool.   • Release of the DAO live funds dashboard: To ensure transparency and trust within the community, Easyzoom will release a live dashboard that tracks all transactions made by the DAO.   • Launch of the first subscription service: launch of a subscription service for individual users.

Launch of the second subscription service: launch of a subscription service that gives access to entire NFT collections.

This stage will guarantee that the community remains in control of the Easyzoom direction and that the community interests remain at the forefront of the project development.

This schedule is a logical progression of steps that will ensure the growth and success of the project. Each step builds upon the previous one, creating a solid foundation for the community to capitalize on the potential of the NFT market. The launch of the lifetime NFT passes and the NFT analysis tool sets the foundation for the community, while the development and improvement of new features, the launch of the subscription service, and the flipping of high-potential NFTs will generate profits for the community.
